About

Get to Know the Man Behind the Code

  • January 2008-2015

    Hospitality

    Growing up in Florida, my first professional career started in 2008 working in the hospitality industry (obviously... I mean Florida right?). I started at a major hotel company and worked my way through the ranks of the front office. Starting as a Night Auditor, then on days as a Guest Services Agent, then to Supervisor, and finally Manager.

  • September 2015-2018

    Revenue Analysis

    I found myself working in Revenue Analysis in 2015. That's when I knew it was time for a career change. After teaching myself advanced excel functionality, I realized how much I enjoyed creating things that made life easier. Efficiency has always been my strength. Finding better ways to do things, especially when I would ask why we do things a certain way and was inevitably told, "Because we always have..."

  • September 2018

    Bootcamp: A New Start!

    It took three years to take the plunge, but I did it. After graduating General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, I never looked back. My new career was being forged with every class I attended, and I loved every second of it.

  • December 2018

    Transition Complete!

    Here I am, a fresh graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. After 13 weeks of non-stop learning, I am still learning. But, the foundation that has been put together is strong, and the sky is the limit when it comes to building my skills!
